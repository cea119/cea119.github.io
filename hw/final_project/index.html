Hey there, I'm Cori!

This is my WebLab final project- which I hope to turn into a passion project . I am a junior majoring in Advertising Management and Interactive Media at the University of Miami. Through this website, I want to create a community and a safe-space to share my personal growth and what has led me there. Too often society and even people who are close to us heavily focus on criticizing us, making us feel insecure, and try to disrupt our flow- as if we don't already do that ourselves.

SO, I want to build a community where we embrace eachother , our ups, our downs and everything in between. We have no time for wack energy so let's focus all of our best energy on designing and building the best versions of ourselves . Throughout different learning opportunities, I came to the conclusion that we spend so too time trying to fix what we "suck" at, rather than embrace what we are good at. So, with that said: ABRAZATE .

What does Abrazate mean?

Abrazate is Spanish for "Hug Yourself." Through this project, I want to learn how to embrace all that I am and help others do so as well. So, welcome to this journey- I promise it won't be boring!
