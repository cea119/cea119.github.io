Miami is unlike any other city in the world. The vibrant city has something for everyone- from its famous night-life, to its white, sandy beaches- Miami promises a good time. Through this "Visit Miami" website, I share a couple of my favorite, hidden Miami spots. Have fun & enjoy Miami!

RESTAURANTS

St.Roch Market, Design District

The St. Roch Market is one of Miami's best food halls. You will find it in the heart of Miami’s Design District- surrounded by some of Miami's most expensive and exclusive stores. The market offers its visitors an extensive and diverse mix of local food and beverages. Filled with Miami talent- the market is a destination for those who appreciate food from different cultures and are willing to try new foods. There are 11 different vendors to chose from. The market is opened all day- it is the perfect place to grab a bite to eat with friends or simply chill with your laptop while getting some work done.

MUSEUMS

Bass Museum of Art, Miami Beach

The Bass is Miami Beach’s contemporary art museum. The museum usually exhibits art made by international artists- representing Miami's diversity.The exhibitions usually incorporate art feauring design, fashion, and architecture.

Institute of Contemporary Art Miami, Miami Design District

The Institute of Contemporary Art, Miami (ICA Miami),located in the trendy Design District, is exhibits contemporary works. Through their numerous exhibitions, ICA Miami prides itself in being a significant and influential platform to promote the work of local, emerging, and under-recognized artists by advancing their public appreciation and exhibiting their innovative art. The museum is deeply committed to providing open, public access to artistic excellence by offering year-round free admission.

NIGHT-LIFE

Basement, Miami Beach

BASEMENT is unlike any place you have visited before. This underground hub in Miami Beach offers entertainment like never before. Here, guests are able to choose among different activies to engage with throughout the night. BASEMENT features a nightclub, bowling alley, and ice-skating rink. Sounds cool, huh! Keep in mind: post 8pm- Basement becomes 21+

Lagniappe, Midtown

“Feel like you've stepped into an old New Orleans parlor from the jazz age” (minus Prohibition, lol). Lagniappe offers its guests live music every night and a wide variety of affordable wines and beers. It is a “cool, low-key” Midtown wine bar, also dishing up simple, grilled American fare; the cozy, homey interior and expansive light-strung “outside listening yard” provide a “great place to make a night of it with friends.”

Visit Lagniappe's Website

Astra, Wynwood

ASTRA is the ideal location for a date or a hang-out with friends. Located on a rooftop- it is the perfect place to enjoy the sunset while drinking some rosé. The lounge has vast seating and always has a good vibe. Each night, a different DJ/live band takes the stage. Recommend FOR SURE!
